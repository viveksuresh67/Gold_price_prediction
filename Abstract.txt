 The goal of this project is to create a predictive model that can predict gold price. The theme of this project can be classified as:
•	Predictive Analytics (Pattern mining, Time-series, Causality, etc.)
•	Data Mining and Knowledge discovery

 
 
 
 
 For this research gold price from 1978 to 2021 has been collected from Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/odins0n/monthly-gold-prices)
